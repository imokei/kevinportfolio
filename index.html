BEBE'S NAILS

During the coronavirus pandemic, my Mom's income was negatively impacted. I designed and created a mobile application for my mom's nail salon to help her keep in contact with her clients. It showcases her work and provide an easy way for customers to contact her which allowed her to earn some extra money.

WWW.ANIME2MANGA.XYZ

Anime fans often finish a season of their favorite show and want more. Luckily for them most animes are adapted from their source manga. However, theres no easy way to find where to continue. I solved this issue by providing a way for fans to easily look up their favorite animes and find out which chapter to continue on.

THE DEX PROJECT

I often wonder what something is and want to learn more about it. I usually google things that I know the name of but the problem occurs when I don't know what something is AND I don't know what it is called. So I created a cross-platform mobile app that allows me to take a picture of it and then "google" search it.

THE STUDENT EXCHANGE

While in University, I worked on a project called the Student Exchange. My team and I wanted to make an easy way for students to buy and sell their used textbooks and unneeded supplies. So to solve this problem we created an online marketplace similar to Amazon but for students in our smaller community.
